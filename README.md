# 📊 Adventure Works Sales & Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Business Intelligence analysis of **Adventure Works Cycles**, a global cycling retailer. Using Power BI, the dashboard uncovers key insights related to sales performance, product trends, customer distribution, and return behavior from **2015 to 2017**.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Evaluate overall sales and profitability
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Analyze regional sales distribution
* Monitor and reduce product return rates

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Data Modeling (Snowflake Schema)
* DAX (for KPIs and calculations)
* Excel / CSV Dataset

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Revenue:** $24.91M
* **Total Profit:** $10.46M
* **Total Orders:** 25,164
* **Return Rate:** 2.17%

---

## 🧩 Data Model Overview

The dataset follows a **Snowflake Schema**, ensuring efficient data organization and scalability.

### Fact Tables:

* Sales Data
* Returns Data

### Dimension Tables:

* Calendar
* Customers
* Territories
* Products

### Relationships:

* One-to-Many (1:*) relationships between dimension and fact tables
* Product hierarchy structured as:
  **Category → Subcategory → Product**

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🔹 Product Performance

* **Top Product (Orders):** Water Bottle - 30 oz. (3,983 orders)
* **Top Product (Profit):** Mountain-200 Black, 46
* **Bikes dominate revenue**, contributing **93% ($9.73M)** of total profit

---

### 🌍 Regional Analysis

* **North America:** 46.59% (Highest sales)
* **Europe:** 29.33%
* **Pacific:** 24.08%

👉 Indicates strong market presence in North America with growth opportunities in other regions.

---

### 🔁 Return Analysis

* **Highest Returns:** Tires and Tubes (529 returns)
* Followed by Bottles and Cages (278 returns)
* **Highest Return Rate:** Bikes (~3%)
* **Lowest Return Rate:** Accessories (~2%)

👉 Suggests quality or expectation gaps in specific product categories.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

* 📈 **Revenue, Profit & Cost Trend**
  Shows consistent growth with a peak in early 2017

* 🌍 **Geographic Sales Map**
  Highlights sales distribution across major regions

* 🌳 **Decomposition Tree**
  Drill-down analysis from Category → Subcategory → Country

---

## 🚀 Business Impact

This dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by:

* Identifying high-revenue products
* Highlighting underperforming regions
* Reducing return rates through insights
* Supporting strategic sales planning
